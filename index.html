    13. 
        

      

    


    

      Security (production requirement): Azure AD SSO. Three access tiers: Rep sees their own appointments within their brand and is the primary dispositioner. Brand Leadership (Sales Manager, Branch Manager, Brand President) sees their brand across all markets and can disposition on behalf of a rep when needed. Corporate (Brand Support Center) — RVPs and above — sees everything, read-only; dispositions remain with the field. Row-level security enforced in the warehouse, not the client. Persona switcher above is a demo affordance — will be removed.
      


      Prototype note: Brand and market lists pulled live from Reports filters. Appointments shown are illustrative; production wiring requires a CRM webhook per source (Acculynx, DataForma, Gorilla Desk, Leap, Salesforce, Zoho/DF) feeding a normalized Appointments table. Submissions queue locally and replay when network returns.
