      Security (production requirement): Azure AD SSO. Three access tiers: Rep sees their own appointments within their brand and is the primary dispositioner. Corporate (Brand Support Center) — RVPs and above — sees everything, read-only; dispositions remain with the field. Row-level security enforced in the warehouse, not the client.
